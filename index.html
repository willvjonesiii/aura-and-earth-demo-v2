Who I Am

From Deep Devotion
to Divine Awakening

The founder of Aura and Earth is a spiritual guide, author, and master practitioner dedicated to resetting the nervous system and empowering women through ancestral healing.

Her journey from a strict religious upbringing to a profound spiritual awakening at age forty informs her deep, compassionate approach to shadow work and somatic restoration. Specializing in African cupping techniques from Zambia alongside traditional divination, she curates luxury international pilgrimages across Southeast Asia designed to release trauma and restore the sacred right to rest.

Her mission is simple yet revolutionary: to teach women how to embrace their gifts and return to the world entirely transformed.

The Mission  ·  Aura & Earth

Aura and Earth blends sophisticated natural elements with ancestral modalities to create space for deep emotional and physical rejuvenation. Led by an author, speaker, and coach, our programs move women through shadow work, road opening rituals, and international wellness pilgrimages.

We believe rest is a radical act of reclamation, and we hold the structure, safety, and wisdom for women to step into their higher purpose without fear.

Your Revolution

Rest is
Your Birthright.

Goddess Aje says it plainly: most women who look like her have never been given permission to stop. To breathe. To take up space without producing something in return.

Rest is not laziness. It is resistance. Resetting the nervous system is the first act of healing. Everything else — the oracle, the cupping, the shadow work, the pilgrimage — flows from that one radical choice.

The pilgrimage is built on this. You travel to Bali, to Vietnam, to Cambodia not to escape your life but to return to it as someone who finally knows how to rest inside of it.

You were never meant to carry this alone.
