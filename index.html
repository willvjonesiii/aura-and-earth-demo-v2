The Healer

Meet Goddess Aje

Healer. Spiritual coach. Apothecary. Artist. Goddess Aje Nafia Holmes carries African cupping she learned in Zambia, the oracle and the egg cleanse, the road opener and the house blessing. Her work is the slow, sacred labor of helping women remember who they were before the world asked them to carry it all.

A pastor's child who became Muslim at eighteen, she began to truly wake at forty. Leaving took years. It felt like being a fish out of water. What she found on the other side became her purpose: to come, and to heal others.

Don't be terrified of your gift. Your purpose is to come, and to heal.
